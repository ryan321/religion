### Lesson 1.1 -- What Is a Law?

Have you ever noticed that when you drop a ball, it always falls down? It never falls up or sideways. It always goes down. That's because of a law called gravity. Laws like gravity work the same way every time, for every person, no matter what.

Did you know that God has laws too? A **law** is a rule or commandment that comes from God and applies to everyone. Just like the law of gravity works whether you believe in it or not, God's laws work the same way. They are always true, and they always have consequences, good or bad.

Think about the **law of honesty**. When you tell the truth, people trust you. When you lie, people stop trusting you. That consequence happens whether you're seven years old or seventy. It works for everyone, everywhere, every time. That's what makes it a law.

In the scriptures, God has given us many laws. Some of them are commandments like "Thou shalt not steal" (Exodus 20:15). Others are principles like the **Word of Wisdom**, which teaches us to take care of our bodies (D&C 89). These aren't just random rules that God made up because He felt like it. Every single law God gives is designed to lead you to **happiness**.

Think of it this way: imagine you got a brand-new bicycle. The bicycle came with instructions: oil the chain, keep the tires full of air, don't ride it off a cliff. Those instructions aren't there to take away your fun. They're there because the person who *made* the bicycle knows exactly how it works best. God made you, and He knows exactly how you work best too. His laws are the instructions for a happy life.

The prophet Alma taught that "wickedness never was happiness" (Alma 41:10). That means breaking God's laws will never make you truly happy, even if it seems fun for a moment. But keeping His laws brings real, lasting joy.

Here's something important to remember: laws are not the same as covenants. A law applies to everyone whether they agree to it or not. You didn't sign up for gravity -- it just works. God's commandments are the same way. They apply to all of His children. In the next lesson, you'll learn about covenants, which are something different and very special.

For now, remember this: God's laws are real, they apply to everyone, and they exist because God loves you and wants you to be happy.
