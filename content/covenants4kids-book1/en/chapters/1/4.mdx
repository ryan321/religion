### Lesson 1.4 -- Why God Uses Laws and Covenants

You might wonder: why does God use laws and covenants at all? Why not just bless everyone and let us figure things out on our own? The answer has everything to do with who God is and how much He loves you.

First, let's be clear about something: God does **not** use laws and covenants to control you. He isn't trying to boss you around or make your life difficult. He's your Heavenly Father, and everything He does is to help you become the best, happiest person you can be.

Think about a good parent. A good parent gives rules (like "eat your vegetables" and "go to bed on time") not because they want to make life boring, but because they know what will help their child grow healthy and strong. God's laws work the same way. They are a **path to happiness**. Every commandment, every standard, every law is designed to guide you toward joy.

But God doesn't stop at laws. He also gives covenants, and this is where things get really beautiful. A covenant creates a **binding relationship** between you and God. It isn't just "follow these rules and you'll be fine." It's "enter into a promise with me, and I will walk with you, strengthen you, and pour out blessings on you."

Why does that matter? Because life is hard. You will face things that are scary, confusing, and painful. Laws alone can tell you the right thing to do, but covenants give you God's power to actually do it. When you are in a covenant relationship with God, you have access to His help, His comfort, and His strength. The Holy Ghost is one of the greatest blessings that comes through covenant, a constant companion to guide and comfort you.

Elder D. Todd Christofferson taught that covenants are not just about what we promise to do; they are about what we become. Through covenants, God is shaping you into someone who can live with Him again someday. The laws show the way, and the covenants give you the power and the relationship to walk that way.

So why does God use laws and covenants? Because He loves you. Laws give you direction. Covenants give you connection. And together, they create a pathway that leads you right back to your Heavenly Father's arms.

That is the whole point, and it has been the point from the very beginning of the world, as you'll see in the chapters ahead.
