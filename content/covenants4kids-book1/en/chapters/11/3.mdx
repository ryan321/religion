### Lesson 11.3 -- The Law of Chastity

Some of God's laws protect your body from things you put into it -- like the Word of Wisdom. But the **law of chastity** protects something even deeper. It protects one of the most sacred powers God has given to His children: the **power of creation** -- the ability to bring new life into the world.

The law of chastity teaches that **sexual intimacy** is only appropriate between a **husband and wife** who are legally and lawfully married. This is not a small rule or an old-fashioned idea. It is one of God's most important laws, and it has been taught by prophets from the beginning of time.

Why does God care so much about this? Because the power to create life is one of the most sacred gifts you will ever receive. Think about it -- God Himself is a Creator. He made the heavens, the earth, and every living thing. And He has shared a portion of that creative power with His children. The ability to bring a new human soul into the world is not ordinary. It is extraordinary. It is divine. And something that sacred deserves a boundary to protect it.

That boundary is the law of chastity. It is not a fence built out of shame or fear. It is a guardrail built out of **love**. God doesn't want you to feel guilty about your body or the feelings you'll experience as you grow up. Those feelings are natural and good -- they are part of God's design. But like any powerful force, they need to be used in the right way, at the right time, and in the right place. Fire can warm your home or burn it down. The difference is whether the fire is inside the fireplace or outside it.

The world sends a very different message. Movies, music, and social media often treat intimacy as casual -- something that doesn't mean much and doesn't need boundaries. But God says the opposite. He says it means everything, and it deserves the highest protection.

The law of chastity is not about saying no to happiness. It's about saying yes to something better -- a kind of love that is protected, committed, and blessed by God. When you live this law, you are honoring the sacred power inside you and preparing yourself for the deepest and most meaningful relationships life can offer.
