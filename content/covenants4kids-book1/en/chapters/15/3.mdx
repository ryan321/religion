### Lesson 15.3 -- All the Laws and Covenants Together

You've learned a lot in this book. Let's take a step back and see how it all fits together, because all of God's laws and covenants are **one connected system**, not a bunch of separate, random rules.

It starts at the very beginning. **Adam** received the first covenants and the first commandments: to worship God, offer sacrifice, and obey. After the flood, God gave **Noah** a covenant with the rainbow as its sign, promising He would never destroy the earth by water again. Then came **Abraham**, who received the great covenant that his descendants would bless all nations and carry the priesthood.

At **Sinai**, God gave Moses the law: the Ten Commandments, rules about worship, and detailed instructions for daily living. The covenant with **David** promised that a king from his line would reign forever, a promise fulfilled by Jesus Christ. And when Jesus came, He established the **new and everlasting covenant**, fulfilling the law of Moses and opening the door to the fulness of the gospel.

Now look at the personal covenants you make on the covenant path. **Baptism** brings you into God's family. The **sacrament** renews that covenant every week. **Priesthood** ordination gives authority to serve. **Tithing** teaches you to put God first with your resources. **Fasting** teaches sacrifice and compassion for the poor. The **Word of Wisdom** teaches you to respect and care for your body. The **law of chastity** teaches you to honor the sacred power of creating life. The **temple endowment** gives you knowledge, covenants, and power from on high. And the **sealing** binds your family together forever.

Do you see it? None of these stand alone. Tithing connects to sacrifice, which connects to consecration. Chastity connects to sealing. The Word of Wisdom connects to honoring the body God gave you. Baptism connects to the sacrament. The priesthood connects to the endowment. Everything is woven together.

God didn't give you a pile of disconnected rules. He gave you a **complete system**, a carefully designed path that covers every part of your life and leads to one destination: **eternal life** with your family in the presence of God. Every law protects you. Every covenant empowers you. And together, they bring you home.
