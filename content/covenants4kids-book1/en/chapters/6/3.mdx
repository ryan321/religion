### Lesson 6.3 -- The Higher Law

The Law of Moses told people what to **do**. The higher law that Jesus taught tells people what to **become**. That's the big difference, and it's what makes the higher law so much more challenging, and so much more powerful.

Let's look at some examples from the Sermon on the Mount in Matthew 5.

The old law said, "Thou shalt not kill" (Exodus 20:13). Pretty clear: don't take someone's life. Most people can follow that rule without too much trouble. But Jesus raised the standard: "Whosoever is angry with his brother without a cause shall be in danger of the judgment" (Matthew 5:22). Now it's not just about your actions. It's about your **heart**. Don't just avoid killing someone. Don't even hold hatred toward them. Deal with the anger inside you before it grows into something worse.

The old law said, "Thou shalt not commit adultery." Jesus said that even looking at someone with lustful thoughts breaks that law in your heart (Matthew 5:28). Again, the standard moved from **behavior** to **heart**.

The old law said, "Love thy neighbour, and hate thine enemy." Jesus completely overturned that: "Love your enemies, bless them that curse you, do good to them that hate you" (Matthew 5:44). This is one of the hardest commandments in all of scripture. Loving people who are kind to you is easy. Loving people who hurt you, insult you, or work against you -- that takes a completely different kind of heart.

And then Jesus gave the ultimate commandment of the higher law: "Be ye therefore **perfect**, even as your Father which is in heaven is perfect" (Matthew 5:48). That word "perfect" can also be translated as "complete" or "whole." Jesus is saying: "Don't just follow a checklist. Become the kind of person God is. Let your character be transformed from the inside out."

Why is the higher law harder? Because it's **deeper**. It's easy to check a box that says "I didn't steal today." It's much harder to root out every bit of selfishness, jealousy, and pride from your heart. The Law of Moses could regulate behavior. The higher law transforms the soul.

But here's the good news: Jesus didn't just give us a harder law and then leave us alone to figure it out. He also gave us the **Holy Ghost**, the **Atonement**, and the **covenants** of the gospel to help us change from the inside. The higher law is harder, but with Christ's help, it's possible.
