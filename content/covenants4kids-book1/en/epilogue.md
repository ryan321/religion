# Epilogue

## You did it!

Great job completing Laws and Covenants for Kids. You now understand something that many adults don't -- the difference between a law and a covenant, and what the major laws and covenants of the gospel actually are.

You started at the beginning -- with Adam, who received the first covenant and the law of sacrifice. You followed the covenant through Noah, Abraham, and Israel at Sinai. You learned what the Law of Moses was really about and how every sacrifice and festival pointed to Jesus Christ. You saw how Christ fulfilled the law and established the higher law -- a law written on the heart, not on stone tablets.

Then you learned about the covenants and laws that shape your life right now. The baptismal covenant -- what you promised and what God promised you. The sacrament -- your weekly chance to renew that covenant. Tithing -- trusting God with your finances. The Word of Wisdom -- trusting God with your body. The law of chastity -- protecting something sacred. And the temple covenants -- the culmination of everything, where families are sealed for eternity.

You are on the covenant path. Every Sunday when you take the sacrament, you are renewing promises that stretch all the way back to Adam. The Abrahamic covenant runs through your veins -- literally or by adoption. The priesthood keys restored by Elijah make your family sealings real.

These are not just words. They are promises. God keeps His promises. And when you keep yours, the blessings are beyond anything you can imagine.

Thank you for learning with us.
