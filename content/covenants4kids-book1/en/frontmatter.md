## Introduction

What's a covenant? What's a law? And why do they matter?

If you've been to church, you've heard these words a hundred times. The baptismal covenant. The law of tithing. The Abrahamic covenant. The Word of Wisdom. Temple covenants. The sacrament. But do you actually know what each one means? When it was given? What you're promising? What God is promising you?

This book explains all of it -- one law, one covenant at a time. No jargon. No complicated theology. Just plain-language explanations of the promises that connect you to God.

You'll start with the basics: what a law is, what a covenant is, and why God uses both. Then you'll walk through the great covenants of the scriptures -- from Adam in the Garden to Abraham in the desert to Israel at Mount Sinai to Jesus Christ and the higher law. Then you'll learn about the covenants and laws that affect your life right now: baptism, the sacrament, tithing, the Word of Wisdom, chastity, and the temple.

By the end, you'll understand the covenant path -- and you'll know exactly where you stand on it.

## How to use this book

This book is different from the narrative history books in this series. Instead of following a story from beginning to end, each chapter covers a specific law or covenant. You can read them in order or jump to the chapter you're most curious about.

The book is designed for 15 weeks, one chapter per week, four lessons per chapter, with a test on the fifth day. Each lesson is about 15-20 minutes.

## Note to parents

This book covers every major law and covenant in the gospel at an age-appropriate level. The chapter on the law of chastity is honest and clear without being graphic. The chapter on temple covenants explains what they are and why they matter without revealing specific wording. The goal is to help your child understand what they're promising (or will promise) and what God promises in return.
