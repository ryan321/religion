### Lesson 11.1 -- What Faith Produces

So far in our study of the Lectures on Faith, we've learned what faith is, what it's based on, and what it requires. Now it's time to ask an important question: **What does faith actually do?**

The answer, according to Lecture 7, is simple and staggering: faith produces **everything**.

That might sound like an exaggeration, but it's not. Think about the creation of the world. How did it happen? God spoke, and the earth was formed. The oceans gathered, the mountains rose, the plants and animals appeared. What powered all of that? **Faith**. God's perfect faith -- His absolute knowledge and power -- brought the entire world into existence. Faith wasn't just a feeling that helped God get through a hard day. It was the **force** that created everything you see around you.

And it didn't stop at creation. Faith is the power behind every great miracle in the scriptures.

When the children of Israel stood trapped between the Egyptian army and the Red Sea, what happened? Moses raised his staff, and the **Red Sea parted** -- not because Moses was magic, but because he exercised faith in the living God.

When people were sick and dying, prophets and apostles **healed** them through faith. When Lazarus had been dead for four days, Jesus **raised him from the dead** through divine power rooted in perfect faith (**John 11:43-44**).

And when Enoch preached to a wicked generation, his faith was so great that his **entire city was translated** -- lifted right off the earth and taken into God's presence (**Moses 7:21**). Think about that. An entire city full of people was taken to heaven because of faith.

The **brother of Jared** had faith so powerful that when he went to the Lord with a problem -- how to light the barges for his people's journey -- the Lord touched the stones he brought, and the brother of Jared actually **saw the Lord's finger** and then His entire body (**Ether 3:6-13**). His faith pierced the veil.

Faith is not just a nice feeling. It is not just a hope that things will work out. It is a **force** -- the most powerful force available to human beings. It moves mountains, parts seas, heals the sick, raises the dead, and draws back the curtain between earth and heaven.

And here's the best part: this force is available to you.
