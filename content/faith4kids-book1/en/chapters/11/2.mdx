### Lesson 11.2 -- Faith and Miracles

The scriptures are absolutely full of miracles. Seas part, fire falls from heaven, blind eyes are opened, and dead people walk out of tombs. These stories are incredible -- but they're not random. Every single miracle in the scriptures was produced by the same thing: **faith**.

Let's walk through some of the most powerful examples.

When the children of Israel were wandering in the wilderness and had no water, they started to complain. The Lord told **Moses** to strike a rock with his staff. Moses obeyed, and **water poured out of solid rock** (**Exodus 17:6**). That wasn't a magic trick. It was the power of faith operating through a man who trusted God completely.

When the prophet **Elijah** stood on Mount Carmel and challenged the priests of Baal, he called down **fire from heaven**. The fire didn't just light the sacrifice -- it consumed the altar, the stones, the dust, and even the water in the trench around it (**1 Kings 18:38**). The priests of Baal had shouted and danced all day and got nothing. Elijah prayed once, and heaven answered. The difference was faith.

In the Book of Mormon, the prophet **Nephi** was given such power through his faith that he could **seal the heavens** -- meaning he could command that no rain would fall, and it wouldn't (**Helaman 10:7**). The Lord trusted Nephi with that power because Nephi's faith was real, grounded, and proven through years of obedience and sacrifice.

And then there's **Jesus Christ** Himself. He **walked on water** in the middle of a storm (**Matthew 14:25**). He **healed the blind**, giving sight to people who had never seen a single color or face (**John 9:6-7**). He stood outside the tomb of His friend Lazarus and called out, "Lazarus, come forth!" -- and a man who had been dead for four days **walked out alive** (**John 11:43-44**).

Every one of these miracles happened because faith is real. Not because the people involved were superhuman or had some secret power. They were ordinary people -- shepherds, farmers, fishermen -- who exercised extraordinary faith in a God who keeps His promises.

That's the lesson. Miracles don't happen because people are special. Miracles happen because **faith is real, grounded, and proven**. And faith like that is available to anyone willing to develop it.
