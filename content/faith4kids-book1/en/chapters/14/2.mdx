### Lesson 14.2 -- How the Lectures Connect to the Temple

If you've ever wondered why the temple is so important, the Lectures on Faith can help you understand. In many ways, the lectures lay the *doctrinal foundation* for what happens in the temple, and the temple brings the lectures to life.

Think about what the Lectures on Faith teach. They walk you through the knowledge of God from the very beginning: the Creation, the Fall, and the plan of redemption through Jesus Christ. They teach you about God's character, His attributes, and His nature. They explain that you need to sacrifice everything to develop true faith. And they teach that the ultimate goal of faith is to **know God personally**, not just know *about* Him, but actually come into His presence.

Now think about what happens in the temple. The **endowment** teaches the same grand story: the Creation, the Fall, and the Atonement of Jesus Christ. It follows the same sequence the lectures discuss, walking you through God's plan step by step. The temple isn't introducing brand-new ideas. It's presenting the same truths the lectures teach, but in a sacred, symbolic, covenant-making setting.

The connections go even deeper. Lecture 6 teaches that faith requires **sacrifice and consecration**, giving everything you have to God. In the temple, you make covenants to do exactly that. You promise to consecrate your time, talents, and everything God has given you to the building of His kingdom. The temple takes the principle from Lecture 6 and turns it into a personal promise between you and God.

And the ultimate goal? The Lectures on Faith teach that the purpose of faith is to bring you into the **presence of God**, to know Him, to be like Him, and to receive everything He has. That's the same goal the temple points you toward. Every ordinance, every covenant, every symbol in the temple is designed to help you come closer to God until you can stand in His presence.

So the Lectures on Faith and the temple are deeply connected. The lectures give you the *understanding*, the knowledge of who God is and how faith works. The temple gives you the *covenants*, the promises and ordinances that put that knowledge into action. Together, they create a path that leads you all the way back to Heavenly Father.

Even if you haven't been to the temple yet, you can prepare right now by studying the truths in the Lectures on Faith. When you learn about God's character, attributes, and nature, you're building the foundation for everything the temple will teach you.
