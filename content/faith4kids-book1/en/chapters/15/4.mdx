### Lesson 15.4 -- Your Faith

The Lectures on Faith were originally written for the **School of the Prophets** -- a group of grown men who were leading the Church during one of the most challenging periods in history. They were missionaries, bishops, and apostles. They were the ones building temples and facing mobs. It might seem like these lectures were meant for them and not for you.

But here's the truth: the principles in the Lectures on Faith are for **everyone**. They're for men and women, old and young, leaders and learners. And they're especially for you -- right now, at your age, in your life.

You don't have to wait until you're an adult to learn about God. You can study His character *now*. You can learn about His attributes *now*. You can come to understand His nature *now*. And the sooner you start building that foundation of knowledge, the stronger your faith will be when the big tests come.

You don't have to wait until you're older to sacrifice either. You make sacrifices every time you choose church over sleeping in, every time you stand up for what's right when it would be easier to stay quiet, every time you give your time or energy to serve someone else. Those aren't small things. Those are the building blocks of real faith.

And you don't have to wait to act. You can pray with real intent today. You can read the scriptures today. You can keep your covenants today. You can choose to trust God with a real problem -- something that actually matters to you -- today. Every time you do, your faith grows.

The Lectures on Faith teach that faith is not for some special class of people. It's not only for prophets or apostles or adults. Faith is **for you**. It's for the kid sitting in Primary. It's for the teenager struggling with doubts. It's for the young person trying to figure out who they are and what they believe. The principles don't change based on your age -- they work the same for everyone.

Here's the most important thing to remember: the sooner you build your faith, the **stronger** it will be when you need it most. Life will bring challenges you can't predict right now. There will be hard days, confusing days, and days when you feel like giving up. But if you've spent years building your faith -- learning about God, sacrificing for Him, and acting on what you know -- you'll have something unshakeable to stand on.

The Lectures on Faith have given you the tools. Now it's up to you to use them. This isn't the end of your journey. It's the beginning. Your faith is yours to build, yours to grow, and yours to live by -- starting today.
