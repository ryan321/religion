## Introduction

You've been told to "have faith" your whole life. But has anyone ever explained what faith actually is?

Not just "believing in something you can't see." That's the Sunday School answer, and it's not wrong -- but it's not the whole picture. Faith is much bigger than that. Faith is the first principle of action in all intelligent beings. It's the force by which God created the world. It's the power behind every miracle in the scriptures. And it's the foundation for every covenant you will ever make.

In 1834-35, Joseph Smith and other Church leaders prepared seven lectures for the School of the Prophets in Kirtland, Ohio. These lectures -- called the Lectures on Faith -- are some of the clearest, most powerful doctrinal teaching in the entire Restoration. They explain what faith is, how it works, what you need to know about God to exercise faith in Him, why sacrifice is essential, and what faith can produce in your life.

Most members of the Church have never read them. This book changes that.

Each chapter takes a concept from the Lectures and explains it in plain language, with examples from the scriptures and from your daily life. By the end, you won't just "have faith" -- you'll understand faith. And understanding it is the first step to using it.

## How to use this book

This book is designed for 15 weeks, one chapter per week, four lessons per chapter, with a test on the fifth day. Each lesson is about 15-20 minutes.

The Lectures on Faith build on each other -- Lecture 1 leads to Lecture 2, which leads to Lecture 3, and so on. Reading the chapters in order is important.

## Note to parents

The Lectures on Faith are theological, but this book makes them accessible to kids. The core ideas -- that faith is active, that it requires knowledge of God, that sacrifice is essential -- are practical and life-changing. This may be one of the most important books in the series, because faith is the foundation for everything else your child will learn and do in the gospel.

You may want to read the original Lectures on Faith alongside this book. They are freely available online and are well worth your time.
