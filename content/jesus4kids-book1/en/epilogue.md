# Epilogue

## You did it!

Great job completing The Ministry of Jesus Christ for Kids. You now know the story of the Savior's life -- not as a collection of separate stories, but as one continuous narrative from Bethlehem to Bountiful and beyond.

You started with the prophecies that pointed to His coming. You followed Him from the manger to the carpenter's shop in Nazareth, to the Jordan River where He was baptized, to the hills of Galilee where He called the Twelve. You heard Him teach on the mountainside, tell parables, and declare "I Am." You watched Him heal the sick, feed the hungry, and raise the dead.

You walked with Him into Jerusalem for the last time. You were there at the Last Supper when He gave us the sacrament. You stood outside Gethsemane while He took upon Himself the sins and sorrows of the world. You saw the trials, the Crucifixion, and the darkness that followed.

And then you saw the empty tomb. You heard Mary's voice when she recognized the risen Lord. You watched Thomas touch the nail prints and say, "My Lord and my God." You followed Jesus across the ocean to the temple in Bountiful, where 2,500 Nephites came forward one by one to feel the wounds in His hands and feet.

That is the story of Jesus Christ. And it is not over.

He lives today. He leads His Church through living prophets. He knows you by name, just as He knew every Nephite child He blessed one by one. Every time you take the sacrament, you are remembering what He did for you. Every time you pray, you are speaking to His Father -- and yours. Every time you keep a covenant, you are walking the path He showed you.

Now that you know His story, keep reading His words. Open the scriptures. Talk to Him in prayer. And watch for His hand in your life -- because the Savior who healed the blind, fed the hungry, and visited the Nephites is the same Savior who is watching over you right now.

Thank you for learning with us.
