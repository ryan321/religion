### Lesson 1.1 -- Joseph of Egypt's Prophecy

Have you ever heard someone predict the future? Maybe a weather forecaster said it would rain, and it did. That is pretty cool. But what if someone predicted something that would happen more than 3,000 years later -- and got it right? That is exactly what happened with a man named **Joseph of Egypt**.

You probably know Joseph from the Old Testament. He was the son of Jacob (also called Israel), the one who was sold into Egypt by his jealous brothers. Joseph became a powerful leader in Egypt and saved his family from starvation. But Joseph of Egypt did something else that matters a lot to our story. He made a **prophecy** -- a prediction given by the power of God -- about someone who would come thousands of years in the future.

The **Book of Mormon** gives us more details about this prophecy than the Bible does. In **2 Nephi chapter 3**, the prophet Lehi shares Joseph of Egypt's words with his own son, also named Joseph. The ancient Joseph said that God would raise up a **"choice seer"** in the last days. This person would be a descendant of Joseph of Egypt himself. And here is the really specific part: "His name shall be called after me; and it shall be after the name of his father" (2 Nephi 3:15).

Think about that. The prophecy said this future prophet would be named Joseph, and his father would also be named Joseph. When **Joseph Smith Jr.** was born on December 23, 1805, his father was **Joseph Smith Sr.** The names matched exactly, just as the prophecy said they would.

The choice seer would do a great work. He would bring forth God's word and help convince people of truths they had already received (2 Nephi 3:11). He would be "great like unto Moses" (2 Nephi 3:9). That is a big comparison. Moses led Israel out of slavery and gave them God's law. The choice seer would lead people out of spiritual darkness and restore God's truth.

Joseph of Egypt saw all of this thousands of years before it happened. He knew that God had a plan and that this plan included a specific person with a specific name. When Latter-day Saints read 2 Nephi 3, they see a direct connection between the ancient prophet Joseph and the modern prophet **Joseph Smith Jr.** The Restoration did not happen by accident. It was part of God's plan from the very beginning.
