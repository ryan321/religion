### Lesson 5.3 -- Moroni's Visits

On the night of September 21, 1823, Joseph Smith was praying in his bed when something extraordinary happened. A **light** appeared in his room, growing brighter and brighter until the room was lighter than noonday. Then a personage appeared, standing in the air beside his bed. He was dressed in a robe of **brilliant whiteness** -- whiter than anything Joseph had ever seen.

The being introduced himself as **Moroni**, a messenger sent from God. He told Joseph that he was a **resurrected being** -- someone who had once lived on the earth, died, and been raised again with a glorified body. Moroni explained that he had been the **last prophet** to write in an ancient record -- a book written on **gold plates** -- that told the history of people who once lived on the American continent.

Moroni said this record contained the **fulness of the everlasting gospel** as it had been delivered by the Savior to those ancient people. The plates were buried in a hill not far from the Smith home, and Joseph had been chosen to bring them forth and translate them.

But Moroni didn't just appear once that night. After delivering his message, the light gathered around him and he ascended back into heaven. Joseph lay there amazed, trying to take it all in. Then the light returned, and **Moroni appeared a second time**, repeating the same message with a few additional details. He left again, and then came back a **third time** -- the same message again, with warnings about the challenges ahead.

Joseph didn't sleep at all that night. Three visits from an angel will do that to you!

The next day, while working in the fields, Joseph was so exhausted that his father told him to go home. On the way, Moroni appeared **yet again** and told Joseph to go tell his father everything. Joseph obeyed, and his father -- good, faithful Joseph Smith Sr. -- believed every word.

Joseph then went to the place Moroni had shown him in vision: **Hill Cumorah**, a large hill near his home. There, under a rounded stone, he found the **gold plates** exactly as Moroni had described. But he was not yet allowed to take them. That part of the story was still to come.
