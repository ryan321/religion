### Lesson 12.3 -- The Waters of Mormon

As more people came to believe Alma's teachings, they needed a place to gather. Alma led them to a spot in the wilderness called the **Waters of Mormon**, a spring of pure water surrounded by a thicket of small trees. During the day, they could hide among the trees and not be seen by Noah's spies (Mosiah 18:5).

It was here that Alma performed some of the most significant **baptisms** in the Book of Mormon. But before he baptized anyone, he taught them exactly what they were committing to. The covenant he described is one of the most beautiful passages in all of scripture.

Alma asked the people if they were "**willing to bear one another's burdens, that they may be light**." He asked if they were willing to "**mourn with those that mourn**" and "**comfort those that stand in need of comfort**." He asked if they were willing to "stand as **witnesses of God** at all times and in all things, and in all places" (Mosiah 18:8-9).

When the people said yes, Alma baptized them in the waters. He went into the water himself and baptized a man named **Helam** first, and then the others followed. The record says that the people were "**filled with the grace of God**" and were "**filled with joy**" (Mosiah 18:14-16).

This is what **baptism** really means. It's not just about going under the water and coming back up. It's about making a **covenant**, a two-way promise with God. You promise to take on Christ's name, remember Him, keep His commandments, and serve the people around you. In return, God promises the companionship of the Holy Spirit and ultimately eternal life.

Notice what Alma emphasized. He didn't just talk about following rules. He talked about how we treat each other: carrying each other's burdens, mourning with the sad, comforting the lonely. Being a follower of Christ means being the kind of person who shows up for other people, especially when life is hard.

The Waters of Mormon became a sacred place, a place of new beginnings for people who had been living under a wicked king and were ready for something better.
