## Introduction

The Book of Mormon is one of the greatest books ever written. But if you've ever tried to read it straight through, you know it can be hard to keep track of everything. Who are all these people? Where are they? When does this happen? How does Zeniff's colony connect to King Benjamin? Why is there suddenly a guy named Alma in the middle of the story about King Noah?

This book answers those questions. It takes the first half of the Book of Mormon -- from 1 Nephi through Mosiah -- and tells it as one continuous story. You'll follow Lehi's family from Jerusalem to the promised land. You'll watch the Nephites and Lamanites split into two civilizations. You'll meet prophets, kings, and ordinary people trying to follow God in difficult circumstances.

Along the way, you'll understand how the different storylines fit together. The story of King Benjamin in Zarahemla and the story of Zeniff's colony in the land of Nephi are happening at the same time. Alma's group and Limhi's group are both trying to get back to Zarahemla. Once you see how the pieces connect, the Book of Mormon becomes a much richer, more exciting read.

Scripture references are included throughout. When you see something like (1 Nephi 3:7) or (Mosiah 18:8-9), that's an invitation to open your Book of Mormon and read the original. This book and the scriptures are meant to go together.

## How to use this book

This book is designed as a workbook for self-study or guided by a parent or teacher. No expertise required -- the lessons explain everything you need to know.

The book is designed to be used over the course of a semester. Each chapter corresponds to a week, with a total of 15 weeks. Each chapter has 4 lessons which are meant to be done once a day, leaving a 5th day for review and the chapter test.

For example, if you started on Monday each week, you could do:

Monday  Chapter 1, Lesson 1.1

Tuesday  Chapter 1, Lesson 1.2

Wednesday  Chapter 1, Lesson 1.3

Thursday  Chapter 1, Lesson 1.4

Friday  Chapter 1, Review and test

Each lesson has questions at the end, and each chapter has a test. Your AI tutor will help you check your answers and talk through anything you're unsure about.

After each lesson, there is a checkbox to mark when the lesson is completed to keep track of progress.

## Note to parents

You don't need to be a Book of Mormon scholar to help your child with this book. The lessons are written directly to the child in plain language. Everything is explained as it comes up.

This book covers 1 Nephi through Mosiah -- roughly the first half of the Book of Mormon narrative. It's designed to help your child understand the timeline, the geography, and the connections between the different groups and storylines. When your child reads the Book of Mormon in Come Follow Me or seminary, they'll already have a mental map of the story.

This is Book 1 of a 3-book series:
- Book 1: From Jerusalem to Zarahemla (1 Nephi - Mosiah)
- Book 2: The Reign of the Judges (Alma - Helaman)
- Book 3: The Coming of Christ and the End (3 Nephi - Moroni, plus the Jaredites)

Each book stands alone, but together they cover the entire Nephite narrative.
