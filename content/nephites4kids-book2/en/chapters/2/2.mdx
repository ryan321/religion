### Lesson 2.2 -- Amulek's Story

When we think about heroes in the scriptures, we often think about prophets and kings. But sometimes the Lord calls **ordinary people** to do extraordinary things. That's exactly what happened with Amulek.

Amulek was not a church leader. He wasn't a prophet, a priest, or a judge. He was a regular man living in the city of Ammonihah. He was well-known in the community and came from a good family. He had a comfortable life with plenty of wealth and many friends. By the world's standards, Amulek had everything (see Alma 10:4).

But Amulek was honest about his spiritual life. He later admitted that even though he knew about God, he had been **"called many times and would not hear"** (see Alma 10:6). He had been ignoring spiritual promptings. He was going along with his comfortable life and not really paying attention to what God wanted him to do. Have you ever felt like that -- like you know what's right but you keep putting it off?

Then everything changed. An **angel** appeared to Amulek and told him to go home because a prophet of God was coming and Amulek needed to receive him. Amulek obeyed, and when Alma arrived, Amulek took him in, fed him, and cared for him (see Alma 10:7-8).

After spending time with Alma, Amulek's heart was completely changed. He went from being a spiritually casual person to someone willing to **give up everything** for the gospel. And that's not just a figure of speech -- Amulek really did lose almost everything. When he stood up with Alma to preach to the people of Ammonihah, his old friends turned against him. His own family rejected him. He lost his wealth and his comfortable life (see Alma 15:16).

Why would he do that? Because Amulek had gained something far more valuable -- a **testimony** of Jesus Christ and a knowledge that the gospel was true. Once he knew the truth, he couldn't stay silent about it, no matter what it cost him.

Amulek's story teaches us two important things. First, you don't have to be a famous church leader to be called by God. He can use anyone who is willing. Second, when we finally **listen** to God's call, even if we've been ignoring it for a while, amazing things can happen. It's never too late to answer.

Amulek became one of the great missionaries in the Book of Mormon -- and it all started because he was willing to open his door and listen.
