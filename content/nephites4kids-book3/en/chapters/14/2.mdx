### Lesson 14.2 -- The Promise

If you could only share one scripture with someone, what would it be? For millions of missionaries around the world, the answer is easy: **Moroni 10:4**. This single verse is probably the most quoted scripture in all of missionary work, and it contains what we call **Moroni's promise**.

Here's what Moroni wrote: "And when ye shall receive these things, I would exhort you that ye would **ask God, the Eternal Father, in the name of Christ, if these things are not true**; and if ye shall ask with a **sincere heart**, with **real intent**, having **faith in Christ**, he will **manifest the truth of it unto you, by the power of the Holy Ghost**" (Moroni 10:4).

Let's break that down, because every part matters.

First, Moroni says to **read** the record. You have to actually study it and think about what it says. You can't ask if something is true if you've never taken the time to learn what it teaches.

Second, you need to **ask God**, not just think about it or talk to a friend, but actually kneel down and pray to Heavenly Father in the name of Jesus Christ. This is a personal, direct conversation with God.

Third, your prayer needs to come from a **sincere heart**. That means you genuinely want to know the truth, not just looking for an excuse to believe or disbelieve. And you need **real intent**, meaning you're willing to act on the answer you receive. It's not a casual question. You have to mean it.

Fourth, you need **faith in Christ**. You need to believe that God can answer you and that Jesus Christ is at the center of this whole message.

And if you do all of that? Moroni promises that God **will** answer. Not might. Not could. **Will**. He will manifest the truth by the power of the Holy Ghost.

This is an extraordinary promise because it makes the Book of Mormon **testable**. It's not asking anyone to just take someone else's word for it. It's saying: here's the book, here's how to find out for yourself, and here's what God will do if you sincerely try. The answer comes directly from God to you.

Millions of people around the world have taken Moroni up on this promise. They've read, they've prayed, and they've received their own personal witness. The promise is still open today, for everyone, including you.
