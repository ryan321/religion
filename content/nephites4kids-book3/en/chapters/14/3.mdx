### Lesson 14.3 -- Spiritual Gifts

Right after giving his great promise, Moroni taught about another important topic: **spiritual gifts**. In Moroni 10:8-18, he listed many of the gifts that God gives to His children -- and he made it clear that these gifts are real and available to everyone who has faith.

What kind of gifts was Moroni talking about? He listed several: the gift of **tongues** (being able to speak or understand languages by the power of the Spirit), the gift of **healing**, the gift of great **faith**, the gift of **prophecy**, the gift of **visions**, and others. These are not ordinary talents like being good at sports or music. These are **supernatural gifts from God**, given through the Holy Ghost to bless and help His children.

Moroni had a strong message for anyone who thought these gifts had disappeared: **"Deny not the gifts of God"** (Moroni 10:8). He taught that spiritual gifts have **not ceased** and will never cease as long as the earth stands and there are people with faith upon it. If the gifts ever seem to disappear, it's not because God stopped giving them -- it's because people stopped having enough faith to receive them.

This was an important teaching for Moroni to include. He knew that by the time people read his record, many centuries would have passed. Many people in the world would believe that the age of miracles was over -- that God used to do amazing things in Bible times but doesn't do them anymore. Moroni wanted to set the record straight: **God is still a God of miracles**.

Think about what this means for you. The same God who parted the Red Sea, who healed the sick during Jesus's ministry, who gave the Nephites incredible spiritual experiences -- that same God is willing to pour out spiritual gifts on **you**. Not because you're perfect, but because you have faith and are willing to receive them.

Moroni taught that every person receives at least **one gift** from God, and that these gifts are given for the benefit of everyone, not just the person who has them. The gift of healing blesses the person being healed. The gift of tongues blesses those who hear the gospel in their own language. Spiritual gifts are meant to be **shared**.

The lesson is clear: we should seek spiritual gifts, be grateful when we receive them, and never deny that God still works miracles among His children. As Moroni declared, wherever there is faith, there will be gifts of the Spirit.
