### Lesson 5.1 -- Healing the Sick

After teaching the people at the temple, Jesus did something that showed exactly who He is. He didn't just talk about love -- He **showed** it.

Jesus told the multitude to bring their **sick**. Bring the lame. Bring the blind. Bring the afflicted. Bring anyone who was suffering in any way. And the people did. They brought everyone who was hurting, everyone who was broken, everyone who needed help. And what did Jesus do?

He **healed every one of them**.

Not some. Not most. Not only the ones who had enough faith or who had earned it through good behavior. **Every single person** who was brought to Him was made whole. This is recorded in **3 Nephi 17:7-10**, and it is one of the most powerful moments in all of scripture.

Think about what this looked like. Imagine a crowd of 2,500 people, and among them are those who cannot walk, who cannot see, who are in pain. One by one, Jesus healed them. The blind opened their eyes. The lame stood up and walked. Those who had been suffering for years were suddenly well. It must have been overwhelming to witness.

And the people's response tells you just how overwhelming it was. The entire multitude -- all 2,500 of them -- **bowed down** at the feet of Jesus. They washed His feet with their tears. They weren't just impressed or grateful. They were overcome. They knew they were in the presence of the Son of God, and they had just watched Him do something no ordinary person could do.

What's important to notice is that Jesus wasn't **selective** about who He healed. He didn't ask people to prove they deserved it. He didn't check their tithing records or ask how often they came to church. He simply said, "Bring them to me." And when they came, He healed them. All of them.

This tells us something important about Jesus Christ. His compassion doesn't have limits. His power doesn't run out. He doesn't help only the people who seem worthy enough. If you are hurting -- physically, emotionally, or spiritually -- His invitation is the same one He gave the Nephites: bring your pain to Him, and He will help you carry it.

The healings at the temple weren't just miracles. They were a message: Jesus Christ sees every person, cares about every person, and has the power to make every person whole.
