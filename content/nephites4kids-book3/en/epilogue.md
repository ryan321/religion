# Epilogue

## You did it!

You've finished the entire History of the Nephites series -- all three books, covering over a thousand years of history from Lehi leaving Jerusalem to Moroni burying the plates in the Hill Cumorah.

Think about what you've seen. You watched a family leave everything behind and cross an ocean on faith. You saw that family divide into two civilizations that would define the next millennium. You met judges, prophets, warriors, and kings. You stood with Abinadi before the wicked King Noah. You marched with the stripling warriors. You watched Captain Moroni raise the Title of Liberty.

You witnessed destruction so complete that the face of the land was changed. And then you saw something no one expected -- Jesus Christ descending from heaven, standing among His people, healing their sick, blessing their children one by one, and weeping with joy.

You followed the Nephites through 200 years of perfect peace and watched them lose it all. You stood with Mormon on the Hill Cumorah as a thousand years of civilization ended in a single day. You walked with Moroni, the last Nephite, as he wandered alone and wrote for a future he would never see.

And you read his promise: ask God if this book is true, and He will answer you.

The Book of Mormon is now more than a book to you. It's a story you know -- with people you've come to care about, lessons that apply to your life, and an invitation that is waiting for your response.

Moroni buried the record. Joseph Smith brought it forth. And now it's in your hands.

What will you do with it?
