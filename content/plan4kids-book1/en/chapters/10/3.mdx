### Lesson 10.3 -- Ordinances Help You Become Like God

In Heavenly Father's plan, ordinances aren't just rituals or checkboxes to mark off a list. Every ordinance is designed to **change you** -- to move you one step closer to becoming like your Heavenly Father. When you understand this, ordinances stop feeling like chores and start feeling like gifts.

Think about **baptism**. When you go under the water, your old life is symbolically washed away. You come up clean, having made a covenant to take upon yourself the name of Christ. That covenant changes your identity -- you aren't just a person trying to be good. You are now someone who belongs to Christ, and He promises to be with you through the Holy Ghost. As the Apostle Paul taught, "Therefore we are buried with him by baptism into death: that like as Christ was raised up from the dead, even so we also should walk in newness of life" (Romans 6:4).

The **sacrament** renews that transformation every week. When you take the bread and water, you're not just remembering Jesus -- you're recommitting to your covenants and receiving a fresh promise that the Spirit will be with you. It's a weekly spiritual reset that keeps the mighty change going in your heart (see Moroni 4:3 and 5:2).

The **endowment** in the temple teaches you about the plan of salvation and gives you power from on high. The word "endowment" literally means "a gift of power." In the temple, you learn sacred things and make covenants that prepare you to live with God. Jesus told His apostles to wait in Jerusalem until they were "endued with power from on high" (Luke 24:49) -- the temple endowment gives that same kind of spiritual power.

**Sealing** ordinances connect you to your family forever. They don't just keep families together -- they connect you to an eternal chain of relationships reaching back to Adam and forward to your children's children. The prophet Malachi said that God would "turn the heart of the fathers to the children, and the heart of the children to their fathers" (Malachi 4:5-6). Sealings make eternal families real.

Each ordinance does something to you. Together, they shape you into the kind of person who can live in God's presence. They aren't hoops to jump through -- they are transformative experiences designed by a loving Father to help you become like Him. When you approach ordinances with this understanding, they become the most powerful experiences of your life.
