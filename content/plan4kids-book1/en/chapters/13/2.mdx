### Lesson 13.2 -- The Millennium

After Jesus Christ returns, the earth will enter a period of one thousand years of peace called the Millennium. The apostle John saw this in vision and wrote that Christ and His people would "live and reign with Christ a thousand years" (Revelation 20:4, 6). For ten centuries, there will be no war, no crime, no hatred, and no suffering caused by wickedness. Imagine a world like that.

One of the most remarkable things about the Millennium is what happens to Satan. He will be bound -- but not with physical chains. The prophet Nephi explains that "because of the righteousness of his people, Satan has no power; wherefore, he cannot be loosed for the space of many years; for he hath no power over the hearts of the people" (1 Nephi 22:26). Satan is bound because people choose to be righteous. His power comes from temptation, and when people stop giving in, he has nothing to work with. This teaches us something powerful: even now, every time you choose the right, you are taking away a little bit of Satan's power in your own life.

During the Millennium, Christ will reign as King over the whole earth. People will still live, work, learn, and grow. Children will be born, families will continue, and there will be great joy. But the contention and violence that have marked so much of human history will be gone entirely.

One of the most important things that will happen during this time is temple work. Think about the billions of people who lived and died without ever hearing the gospel or receiving the saving ordinances. During the Millennium, temple work will move forward at an extraordinary pace so that every person who ever lived will have the chance to receive baptism, confirmation, and sealing through the work done on their behalf. This is how the plan is perfectly just and perfectly merciful -- no one is left out.

The earth itself will be changed. The tenth Article of Faith teaches that "the earth will be renewed and receive its paradisiacal glory." The earth will be restored to a beautiful, Edenic state -- the way it was before the Fall. Deserts will bloom, animals will be at peace, and the whole creation will reflect the glory of its Creator.

The Millennium is not just a nice idea or a dream. It is a real part of Heavenly Father's plan -- and it is coming. Every person on earth during that time will live in a world governed by perfect fairness and perfect love, with the Savior Himself at the head.
