### Lesson 14.3 -- Free and Open to Everyone

One of the most important truths about Heavenly Father's plan is that it is not reserved for a special few. It is not limited to people who were born in the right country, the right family, or the right time period. The prophet Nephi declared it boldly: "He inviteth them all to come unto him and partake of his goodness; and he denieth none that come unto him, black and white, bond and free, male and female; and he remembereth the heathen; and all are alike unto God" (2 Nephi 26:33).

Read that again carefully. All are alike unto God. Every single person who has ever lived -- regardless of when they were born, where they grew up, what language they spoke, or what challenges they faced -- has a place in this plan. No one is left out. No one is overlooked. No one is denied.

But what about the billions of people who lived and died without ever hearing about Jesus Christ or the gospel? Is the plan unfair to them? Not at all. That is why the spirit world exists, and that is why we do temple work for the dead. Every person who did not have the chance to hear and accept the gospel in this life will receive that chance in the spirit world. And through proxy baptisms, confirmations, and sealings performed in the temple, those who accept the gospel will receive every ordinance they need. Nothing is withheld.

This is how the plan is perfectly just and perfectly merciful at the same time. Justice demands that no one be punished for something they never had the chance to learn. Mercy provides a way for everyone to receive the blessings of the Atonement. And the combination of spirit world teaching and temple ordinances means that literally every person who has ever been born will have a full and fair opportunity to accept the plan.

Think about what this means. A child born in a remote village thousands of years ago has the same access to salvation as someone born into a faithful Latter-day Saint family today. The timing is different, the path looks different, but the opportunity is the same. That is what makes this plan divine. No human plan could ever be this thorough, this fair, or this loving.

Heavenly Father designed a plan that excludes no one who is willing to come. Every door is open. Every invitation is extended. The plan of salvation is truly free and open to everyone.
