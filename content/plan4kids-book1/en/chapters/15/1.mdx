### Lesson 15.1 -- Where You Came From

You did not begin at birth. Long before you took your first breath on this earth, you existed. You lived. You thought, you felt, and you made choices that brought you to where you are right now.

The scriptures teach that every person has an intelligence -- a core of who you are -- that has existed forever. Heavenly Father took that intelligence and organized you into a spirit, and you became His spirit child. You lived with Him in the premortal world. You knew Him. You loved Him. And He knew and loved you.

In that premortal life, Heavenly Father called a great council in heaven. He presented His plan -- the plan of salvation -- and explained that His children would need to leave His presence, come to earth, receive physical bodies, and be tested. It would be hard. There would be pain and sorrow and struggle. But it was the only way to grow and become like Him.

Two volunteers stepped forward. Jesus Christ offered to come to earth, live perfectly, suffer for the sins of all mankind, and give all the glory to the Father. Lucifer offered a different plan -- one that would take away everyone's agency and force them to obey, with the glory going to himself. The Father chose Jesus, and a war broke out in heaven.

You were there. You heard both plans. And you chose. You chose the Father's plan. You sustained Jesus Christ as your Savior. You fought on the right side in the war in heaven, standing for agency, growth, and the freedom to choose. And you won.

Then you chose to come to earth. You knew it would be difficult. You knew you would forget your premortal life. You knew you would face temptation, pain, and uncertainty. But you also knew the plan was worth it -- that the blessings waiting on the other side were greater than anything you would have to endure. You came here on purpose, sent by a Father who loves you.

You are not here by accident. You are not a random collection of atoms that happened to come together. You are a child of God who chose to be here, and that changes everything about how you see yourself and the life you're living right now.
