### Lesson 15.2 -- Why You're Here

You are on earth for a reason. Mortality is not random, and your life is not an accident. You are here because a loving Heavenly Father designed a plan, and you are right in the middle of it.

One of the first reasons you are here is to receive a physical body. In the premortal life, you were a spirit. Your body is a gift, a sacred gift, that allows you to experience things that spirits simply cannot. You can touch, taste, run, create, and feel in ways that are only possible with a body. Your body is an essential part of becoming like Heavenly Father, who also has a glorified body of flesh and bone.

Another reason you are here is to be tested and to grow. Heavenly Father didn't send you to earth so you could coast through life on easy street. He sent you here so you could become stronger. The prophet Lehi taught that "there must needs be an opposition in all things" (2 Nephi 2:11). Without hard things, you could never develop patience. Without sorrow, you could never fully appreciate joy. Without temptation, your choice to do right would mean nothing. Opposition is not a flaw in the plan; it is the plan. Struggle is how you grow.

Agency is at the center of everything. You are here so that you can truly choose -- not be forced, not be programmed, but genuinely decide who you will become. Every day you face choices between right and wrong, kindness and cruelty, faith and doubt. Those choices are real, and they shape your eternal character.

But you are not left alone. Heavenly Father surrounded you with help. You have the gift of the Holy Ghost to guide you. You have covenants and ordinances that bind you to the Savior. You have the scriptures, prophets, and the examples of faithful people around you. You have prayer, a direct line to your Father in Heaven anytime you need Him.

Your life is not random. Every experience, the joyful ones and the painful ones, is part of a plan designed by a Father who loves you more than you can imagine. He didn't send you here to fail. He sent you here to learn, to grow, and to prove that the person you are becoming is the person you truly want to be.
