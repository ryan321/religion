### Lesson 15.4 -- A Perfect Plan

We have journeyed through the entire plan of salvation together -- from the premortal life, through the Creation, the Fall, mortality, the Atonement of Jesus Christ, the spirit world, the Resurrection, the kingdoms of glory, the Millennium, the Final Judgment, and the promise of eternal families. Now it is time to step back and see it all at once, because when you do, something remarkable becomes clear: this plan is perfect.

It is **perfectly just**. Every law in the plan has a consequence. Every choice matters. No one is punished for something they didn't do, and no one escapes accountability for something they did. Justice is never violated -- not once, not ever. God's fairness is absolute and complete.

It is **perfectly merciful**. The Savior Jesus Christ paid the price for every sin, every sorrow, and every pain so that mercy could be extended to everyone who repents. Mercy does not destroy justice -- it satisfies it. The Savior took the punishment upon Himself so that you could be forgiven and made clean. His sacrifice is infinite, and it reaches every person who has ever lived.

It is **perfectly fair**. Every person who has ever been born will have a full opportunity to hear the gospel, accept it, and receive every saving ordinance -- whether in this life or through the spirit world and temple work performed on their behalf. No one is left out because of where or when they were born. The plan accounts for every person in every circumstance.

It is **free and open to all**. The invitation to come unto Christ is extended to everyone, everywhere. As the prophet Nephi wrote, "He denieth none that come unto him" (2 Nephi 26:33). There is no list of people who are excluded. There is no door that is locked. Everyone is welcome.

And it is **personally yours**. Heavenly Father did not create the plan for humanity as a faceless crowd. He made it for you. The Savior did not suffer in Gethsemane for billions of anonymous souls -- He suffered for you, individually, by name. The Holy Ghost does not guide "people in general" -- He guides you, personally, every day. You are never alone in this plan.

This is the greatest truth you will ever learn. You are a child of God. You came from His presence. You are here on purpose. You have a Savior who loves you beyond anything you can imagine. And your future -- if you choose it -- is brighter and more glorious than anything this world can offer.

Now go live it.
