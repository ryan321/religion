### Lesson 2.1 -- You Lived Before

Before you were born on this earth, before your parents ever held you, before you took your first breath, you were already alive. You lived as a spirit in the presence of your Heavenly Father. This time is called the **premortal life**, and it was real.

The prophet Jeremiah learned this in a powerful way. God told him, "Before I formed thee in the belly I knew thee; and before thou camest forth out of the womb I sanctified thee, and I ordained thee a prophet unto the nations" (Jeremiah 1:5). God didn't say, "I will get to know you after you're born." He said, "I already knew you." That means Jeremiah existed before he was born, and so did you.

In the premortal life, you had a **spirit body**. It looked like your physical body, but it was made of spirit matter, which is finer and purer than the physical matter we can see and touch (D&C 131:7-8). You also had a **personality**. You weren't a blank slate waiting to be filled in. You were you, with your own thoughts, your own feelings, your own way of seeing things.

Most importantly, you had **agency**, the ability to make choices. You could learn, grow, and decide for yourself what kind of person you wanted to be. The premortal life wasn't a waiting room where nothing happened. It was an active, real period of your existence where you were learning and developing.

You lived with Heavenly Father and with billions of other spirit children. Jesus Christ was there. He was the Firstborn of the Father's spirit children (Colossians 1:15). You knew Him. You learned from Him. You were part of a great family.

Think about how different your life feels when you know this. You're not a stranger who showed up on earth by accident. You're a child of God who lived with Him, learned from Him, and was sent here for a reason. You had a life before this life, and the things you learned and the choices you made there helped shape who you are now.

Understanding the premortal life changes everything. It means your birth wasn't your beginning. It was your next step. You came to earth not because your existence was just starting, but because you were ready for the next stage of the plan.
