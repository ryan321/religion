### Lesson 2.3 -- Agency in the Premortal Life

One of the most important things you had in the premortal life was **agency**, the power to choose for yourself. You weren't a puppet. You weren't programmed. You made real choices, and those choices mattered.

Abraham 3:22-23 gives us a glimpse of what this looked like. God saw among the intelligences "that they were good," and He noticed that some were "more intelligent than they all." Some spirits were "noble and great." This tells us something very important: not everyone was the same. Some spirits used their agency to be more faithful, more diligent, and more devoted than others.

Does that mean God loved some of His children more than others? No. But it does mean that the choices spirits made in the premortal life had real consequences. Those who were especially faithful were **foreordained** to special missions on earth. Abraham was told that he was one of those chosen before he was born. Jeremiah was told the same thing (Jeremiah 1:5). Being foreordained didn't mean they had no choice on earth. It meant they had earned trust through their premortal faithfulness, and God gave them important responsibilities.

Here's the key idea: **nobody was forced**. Heavenly Father didn't make anyone be faithful. He didn't program some spirits to be more obedient than others. Each spirit child chose for themselves how they would respond to the Father's teachings, to His love, and to His plan. Agency was just as real in the premortal life as it is now.

And this brings us to the most important choice ever made in the premortal life: when Heavenly Father presented His plan, **you chose to follow it**. You heard the plan. You understood that it would be hard, that there would be pain, temptation, and the risk of failure. And you chose to come anyway. In fact, the scriptures tell us that all the spirit children who accepted the plan "shouted for joy" when they learned they would have the chance to come to earth (Job 38:7).

Think about that. You didn't come to earth kicking and screaming. You came because you chose to, and you were excited about it. You believed in the plan. You trusted Heavenly Father. And even though mortality is hard, the choice you made to be here was one of the best choices you've ever made.
