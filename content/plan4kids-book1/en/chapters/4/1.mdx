### Lesson 4.1 -- Lucifer's Rebellion

In the Council in Heaven, Heavenly Father presented His plan -- the plan of salvation. He explained that His children would go to earth, receive bodies, and be tested. Jesus volunteered to be the Savior, saying He would follow the Father's plan and give all the glory to God. But not everyone agreed.

Lucifer, another spirit son of God, was angry at the Father's decision. He didn't just disagree quietly -- he rebelled openly against God. The scriptures tell us that "there was war in heaven: Michael and his angels fought against the dragon; and the dragon fought and his angels" (Revelation 12:7-9). This wasn't a war with swords and shields. It was a war of influence, testimony, and choice. Spirits on both sides used words and persuasion to convince others to join them.

Michael -- who would later come to earth as Adam -- led the faithful spirits who supported the Father's plan and chose Jesus as their Savior. Lucifer led those who rejected the plan and followed him instead.

What made this war so important is that every single spirit had to choose. There was no sitting on the fence, no "I'll decide later." Every son and daughter of God took a side. Some chose to follow Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ. Others chose to follow Lucifer. This was one of the most important decisions ever made -- and it happened before any of us came to earth.

Think about how big that choice was. These were real spirits -- your brothers and sisters -- choosing between two very different paths. One path offered growth, freedom, and the chance to become like God, even though it came with risks. The other path promised something that sounded safe but came at a terrible cost, which we'll learn about in the next lesson.

The war in heaven teaches us that choosing the right has always mattered. Even before this life, standing up for truth required courage. And the fact that you are here on earth right now means something incredible about the choice you made.
