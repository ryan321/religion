### Lesson 4.3 -- One-Third Cast Out

The war in heaven ended, but not everyone chose the right side. The scriptures tell us that a huge number of spirits followed Lucifer in his rebellion. In Doctrine and Covenants 29:36, the Lord explains that "the third part of the hosts of heaven turned he away from me because of their agency." One-third of all God's spirit children chose to reject the Father's plan and follow Satan.

The book of Revelation describes this same event with powerful imagery: "His tail drew the third part of the stars of heaven, and did cast them to the earth" (Revelation 12:4). Those "stars" represent the spirits who followed Lucifer. They were cast out of heaven and sent to earth, but not in the way we were sent. They were cast down without the privilege of receiving physical bodies.

This is one of the most serious consequences of their choice. Every spirit who followed Lucifer was denied a physical body forever. They will never be born, never experience what it's like to touch, taste, smell, run, laugh, or grow in a body. They became Satan and his devils (sometimes called demons or evil spirits) and they have been working to tempt and oppose God's children throughout all of history.

Think about what they lost. They can never progress. They can never have families. They can never repent and return to Heavenly Father. Their decision in the premortal life was final. They chose Lucifer, and now they are stuck on that path forever.

But here's something important to remember: their ultimate defeat is certain. Satan and his followers may cause trouble and temptation in this life, but they have already lost the war. Jesus Christ has already won through His Atonement. At the end of it all, Satan and his followers will be cast out forever, and they will have no more power over God's children.

The story of the one-third is both sad and hopeful. It's sad because so many of our spirit brothers and sisters made that terrible choice. But it's hopeful because it reminds us that we didn't. We chose the right side, and because of that, we have the incredible opportunity to be here on earth with a body, learning and growing toward eternal life.
