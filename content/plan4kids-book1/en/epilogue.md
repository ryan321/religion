# Epilogue

## You did it!

Great job completing The Plan of Salvation for Kids. You now understand the most important doctrine in the entire gospel -- the plan that explains everything.

You started before the beginning. You learned that you existed as an intelligence, became a spirit child of God, and lived with your Heavenly Father before you were born. You were there at the council in heaven when the Father presented His plan. You heard Jesus volunteer to be the Savior. You chose the Father's plan. You fought on the right side in the war in heaven. And you chose to come to earth.

You learned why the earth was created -- so you could receive a body, be tested, and grow. You understood the Fall -- not as a disaster but as a necessary doorway. You saw why a Savior was needed and how justice and mercy work together without either one being robbed. The Savior paid the price that justice demanded so that mercy could reach you.

You learned about the Atonement -- the central act of the entire plan. You understood that resurrection is a free gift for everyone and that repentance is available to everyone who comes to Christ. You learned how ordinances and covenants change you, how being born again transforms your heart, and how you can become a joint heir with Christ and receive all that the Father has.

You explored the spirit world and learned that everyone gets a fair chance -- through teaching in the spirit world and temple work on earth. You studied the three degrees of glory and understood that God provides a place for everyone. You looked ahead to the Second Coming, the Millennium, the final judgment, and the day the earth becomes a celestial kingdom.

And you learned the most beautiful truth of all: the plan is perfectly just, perfectly merciful, and personally yours. You are not an afterthought. You are not a number. You are a child of God with a divine nature and an eternal destiny. The Savior suffered for you. The Father designed the plan for you. And the Holy Ghost is with you right now, helping you walk the path home.

This is your plan. Now go live it.

Thank you for learning with us.
