## Introduction

This is the most important book in the series.

Not because the other books aren't important -- they are. But every other book exists because of this one. The priesthood exists because of the plan. Covenants exist because of the plan. The temple exists because of the plan. The Atonement, the Restoration, the scriptures, the Church -- all of it exists because Heavenly Father has a plan for His children. And this book explains that plan from beginning to end.

You'll start before the beginning -- with intelligences and the premortal life, where you lived with God as His spirit child. You'll learn about the council in heaven, where two plans were proposed and you chose to follow Christ. You'll understand the war in heaven, the Creation, the Fall, and why a Savior was needed.

Then you'll learn how the plan works in your life right now -- how laws, covenants, and ordinances help you progress, how being born again changes you from the inside, and how the Savior makes up the difference when you fall short.

And you'll look ahead -- to the spirit world, the resurrection, the degrees of glory, the Millennium, and the final judgment, when the earth itself becomes a celestial kingdom.

The plan is perfectly just. It is perfectly merciful. It is free and open to every person who has ever lived. And it is personally, specifically yours.

## How to use this book

15 chapters, one per week, four lessons per chapter, test on the fifth day. Each lesson is about 15-20 minutes. Scripture references throughout -- keep your scriptures handy.

## Note to parents

This is the foundational doctrinal book of the series. Every other book -- the priesthood, the covenants, the Restoration, the scriptures -- connects back to the plan of salvation. This is a great book to start with or to read alongside the others. The plan of salvation is the framework that makes everything else make sense.
