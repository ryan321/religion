### Lesson 10.1 -- What the Oath and Covenant Is

Of all the promises God has ever made to His children, the **oath and covenant of the priesthood** may be the most sweeping and remarkable. It is found in Doctrine and Covenants 84:33-44 and describes a two-way agreement between God and those who receive the Melchizedek Priesthood.

Let's break down the words first. A **covenant** is a sacred promise between you and God. Both sides agree to do something. An **oath** takes it even further. It is the strongest kind of promise that exists. When God makes an oath, He is saying, "I guarantee this with everything I am." People break promises sometimes, but God never breaks His oath. Ever.

So what does this covenant say? On your side, you promise two things. First, you covenant to **magnify your calling** -- meaning you take your priesthood duties seriously, serve faithfully, and grow into your responsibilities. Second, you promise to **live by every word that proceeds from the mouth of God** (D&C 84:44). That means following the commandments, listening to the prophets, and letting the scriptures guide your life.

On God's side, He promises something almost too big to believe. In Doctrine and Covenants 84:38, the Lord says that the faithful will receive "all that my Father hath." Read that again. **All that my Father hath.** Not some blessings. Not a nice reward. Everything. This is the promise of **exaltation**: becoming like God, receiving His glory, His power, and His eternal life. And God doesn't just promise it; He swears it with an **oath**, the most binding guarantee in the universe.

Why does this matter to you right now? Because the Aaronic Priesthood you hold as a young man is preparing you for this covenant. Every time you pass the sacrament reverently, every time you fulfill a duty faithfully, you are building the kind of character that will help you one day enter into, and keep, this most sacred agreement.

No other promise in all of scripture is quite like this one. God is telling you that if you do your part, He will give you literally everything He has. That is the oath and covenant of the priesthood.
