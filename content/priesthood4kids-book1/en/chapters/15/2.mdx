### Lesson 15.2 -- Adam-ondi-Ahman

There is a place in Missouri called **Adam-ondi-Ahman**. Today it looks like quiet, rolling hills and farmland. But according to the scriptures, this place has an incredible past, and an even more incredible future.

Doctrine and Covenants 116 identifies Adam-ondi-Ahman as the place where Adam -- the first man and the first priesthood holder on earth -- gathered his righteous posterity and blessed them near the end of his life. D&C 107:53-56 gives us a remarkable picture of that event: "Three years previous to the death of Adam, he called Seth, Enos, Cainan, Mahalaleel, Jared, Enoch, and Methuselah, who were all high priests, with the residue of his posterity who were righteous, into the valley of Adam-ondi-Ahman." There, "the Lord appeared unto them, and they rose up and blessed Adam, and called him Michael, the prince, the archangel."

What an amazing scene. Adam, the father of the human family, surrounded by his righteous descendants, all high priests, being visited by the Lord Himself.

But this is not just ancient history. The prophet Daniel saw a vision of something that will happen in the future at this same place. In Daniel 7:9-14, Daniel described the **"Ancient of Days"** sitting on a throne, with thousands upon thousands serving before him. Then he saw **"one like the Son of Man"** (Jesus Christ) coming to receive dominion, glory, and a kingdom that "shall not be destroyed."

Church leaders have taught that before the **Second Coming**, a **grand council** will be held at Adam-ondi-Ahman. At this council, Adam (who is the Ancient of Days) will gather together all the prophets and priesthood leaders from every dispensation. Each one will report on his stewardship. And then Adam will do something breathtaking: he will **return all priesthood keys to Jesus Christ**, the rightful owner of all priesthood power.

This is one of the great events of the future, a moment when all of history's priesthood holders will stand before the Savior and the full story of God's work on earth will come together in one place.

Adam-ondi-Ahman isn't just a spot on a map. It is a place where the past, present, and future of the priesthood all come together.
