### Lesson 15.4 -- The Priesthood Is Eternal

Here is something that might surprise you: the priesthood will **not end** when Jesus Christ returns. It will not be retired or shut down. The priesthood is eternal. It existed before the world was created, it operates now, and it will continue **forever**.

Think back to what you learned at the beginning of this book. The priesthood is God's power. And since God is eternal, His power is eternal too. Doctrine and Covenants 84:17 teaches that the priesthood "continueth in the church of God in all generations, and is without beginning of days or end of years." It has no start date and no expiration date.

Before the earth was formed, Heavenly Father organized and planned. Abraham saw in vision that God stood among the noble and great spirits and said, "These I will make my rulers" (Abraham 3:22-23). The priesthood was part of the plan from the very beginning, before any of us came to earth.

So what happens to the priesthood after Christ returns and the earth is glorified? For those who receive **exaltation** in the **celestial kingdom**, the priesthood continues as part of their eternal lives. The Lord promised in Doctrine and Covenants 84:38, "He that receiveth me receiveth my Father; and he that receiveth my Father receiveth my Father's kingdom; therefore all that my Father hath shall be given unto him." All that the Father has, including His power, His priesthood, and His glory.

This means the priesthood is not just a Church program. It is not a temporary assignment that ends when mortality is over. It is **how God works**. It is how worlds are created, how lives are blessed, how families are sealed, and how glory is shared. And for those who are faithful, it is how **you** will work with God forever.

Imagine holding the same power that organized the stars and the seas, not because you earned it on your own, but because a loving Father invited you to share in everything He has. That is the promise of the priesthood. That is the covenant of exaltation.

The priesthood stretches from eternity past to eternity future. And right now, right where you are, you are learning about it, preparing for it, and beginning to live it. What an incredible privilege. The priesthood is not just part of your life today. If you are faithful, it will be part of your life forever.
