### Lesson 2.1 -- Heavenly Father and the Priesthood

Where did the priesthood come from? Did someone invent it? Was there a day when it didn't exist? The answer is no. The priesthood is **eternal**. It has always existed because God has always existed. They are inseparable.

The priesthood isn't a tool that Heavenly Father picked up one day and decided to use. It is part of who He is. **Everything** God does (creating worlds, organizing matter, governing the universe, blessing His children) He does through the priesthood. The Prophet Joseph Smith taught that the priesthood is "the channel through which all knowledge, doctrine, the plan of salvation, and every important matter is revealed from heaven" (Teachings of the Prophet Joseph Smith).

Think about that for a moment. When God created the earth, He used priesthood power. When He organized the plan of salvation, it was through the priesthood. When He speaks to prophets, the priesthood is the channel. The priesthood isn't separate from God. It **is** the way God works.

Doctrine and Covenants 84:17 teaches that the priesthood "continueth in the church of God in all generations, and is without beginning of days or end of years." Without beginning. Without end. That's what eternal means. The priesthood didn't start with Moses or Abraham or even Adam. It existed before any of them were born because it existed with God before this world was formed.

So why does Heavenly Father share this incredible power with people on earth? Because He loves us, and because His work requires hands and voices here below. As He told Moses, "This is my work and my glory -- to bring to pass the immortality and eternal life of man" (Moses 1:39). God could do everything Himself, but He chooses to let His children participate in His work. That's what the priesthood is: an invitation to help God with His most important purposes.

When Heavenly Father shares the priesthood with someone, He is literally sharing His own means of accomplishing His work. He holds the **fulness** of priesthood power. Every prophet, apostle, elder, and deacon who has ever held the priesthood received their authority from Him. It all flows from one source: our Father in Heaven.

Understanding this changes how you see the priesthood. It's not just a church program. It's not just something men do on Sundays. It is the very power of the God who created you, shared with you because He trusts you to help with His work.
