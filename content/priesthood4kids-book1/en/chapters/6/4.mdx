### Lesson 6.4 -- The Dispensation of the Fulness of Times

We live in a dispensation unlike any other. It is called the **Dispensation of the Fulness of Times**, and it is the final chapter in God's plan before the Savior returns. Doctrine and Covenants 112:30 declares that the Lord is calling servants "to whom the keys of the power of the holy priesthood have been given" in this "dispensation of the fulness of times."

Why is it called the "fulness" of times? Because **every key, every ordinance, and every truth** from every previous dispensation has been **restored** in this one. Nothing is missing. The Aaronic Priesthood that John the Baptist restored? Here. The Melchizedek Priesthood that Peter, James, and John brought back? Here. The gathering keys from Moses, the Abrahamic covenant keys from Elias, the sealing keys from Elijah? All here. This dispensation contains everything.

The Apostle Paul foresaw this gathering of all things. In Ephesians 1:10, he wrote about God's plan "that in the dispensation of the fulness of times he might gather together in one all things in Christ, both which are in heaven, and which are on earth." This is exactly what is happening now. Temple work connects the living and the dead. Missionary work gathers Israel from every nation. The gospel is going to the whole world.

This dispensation is also different for another critical reason: it **will not end in apostasy**. Every previous dispensation ended with people falling away and priesthood keys being lost. But the Lord has promised that this time, the keys will remain on the earth until Jesus Christ Himself returns. Daniel 2:44 prophesied that God would "set up a kingdom, which shall never be destroyed." That kingdom is The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, and it is rolling forward.

Think about what that means for you. You are living in the greatest dispensation in all of history. You have access to every ordinance, every covenant, every key, and every truth that God has ever given to any prophet. The scriptures from every era are in your hands. The temples are open. The priesthood is fully restored.

This is not a time to sit on the sidelines. The Lord needs every young person to understand what has been given to this generation and to step forward in faith. You are part of the final chapter, and it is the most important one.
