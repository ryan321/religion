### Lesson 7.4 -- Are Any Keys Still Missing?

We have learned about the restoration of the Aaronic Priesthood, the Melchizedek Priesthood, and the keys brought by Moses, Elias, and Elijah in the Kirtland Temple. That is a lot of heavenly messengers and a lot of authority given back to the earth. But here is an interesting question: Has everything been restored? Are there any keys still missing?

The Apostle Paul taught that God's plan is to gather "together in one all things in Christ, both which are in heaven, and which are on earth" (Ephesians 1:10). This is called the "dispensation of the fulness of times," a period when everything from every previous era would be brought together. Church leaders have taught that all the priesthood keys necessary for the salvation and exaltation of God's children have been restored and are held by the living prophet and the Quorum of the Twelve Apostles today.

But the scriptures also describe future events connected to the priesthood that have not yet taken place. For example, the scriptures speak of a time when "the sons of Levi" will again "offer unto the Lord an offering in righteousness" (D&C 13:1; see also Malachi 3:3). There is also the great council at Adam-ondi-Ahman described in Daniel 7:9-14 and Doctrine and Covenants 116, where Adam will meet with his posterity and the Savior will receive His rightful dominion over the earth.

What do these future events mean? Some Church leaders have suggested they may involve keys or authority not yet given. Others believe they represent the culmination and full exercise of keys that have already been restored but not yet fully used. Either way, the Lord will reveal what is needed in His own time and in His own way.

What we know for certain is this: the priesthood authority needed to perform saving ordinances (baptism, confirmation, ordination, temple endowment, and sealing) is on the earth right now. The keys are held by the prophet and apostles, and they are passed from one generation of leaders to the next in an unbroken chain, just as God intended. As the Lord promised in Doctrine and Covenants 65:2, "The keys of the kingdom of God are committed unto man on the earth, and from thence shall the gospel roll forth unto the ends of the earth."

We can trust that the Lord has given us what we need and that He will continue to reveal more as the time is right.
