### Lesson 10.2 -- The Spiritual Outpouring

The dedication of the Kirtland Temple was not just a single event. In the weeks surrounding the dedication, the Saints experienced an incredible **spiritual outpouring** unlike anything most of them had ever known. It was a season of miracles.

During meetings held in and around the temple, people received **visions**. Some spoke in **tongues** -- languages they had never learned -- and others interpreted what was said. Members stood up and delivered **prophecies** about the future of the Church and the work of God. Several people reported seeing **angels** present in the room during worship services. The Spirit of the Lord was being poured out in abundance.

**Joseph Smith** himself said that what was happening reminded him of the **Day of Pentecost** described in the New Testament (Acts 2). In that Bible story, the apostles of Jesus Christ gathered together after His ascension. The Holy Ghost came upon them with great power. They spoke in tongues, prophesied, and thousands were converted. Joseph said the Saints in Kirtland were experiencing something very similar.

Why did this happen? Think about what the Saints had done to get to this point. They had **sacrificed everything** to build the temple. Many gave their last coins. Women donated their fine china to be crushed and mixed into the plaster so the temple walls would sparkle. Men worked through freezing winters hauling stone and timber. They gave up comfort, time, and worldly goods because the Lord had asked them to build His house.

And the Lord kept His promise. When His people sacrificed to obey His commandments, He **poured out blessings** on them. The spiritual experiences at Kirtland were not random. They were the direct result of faithful obedience.

This teaches an important principle. When we give our best to the Lord -- even when it is hard -- He does not forget us. He blesses us in ways we might not expect. The Saints in Kirtland learned that **sacrifice brings blessings**. They had given up earthly things, and in return, the Lord gave them heavenly experiences that strengthened their faith for the difficult years ahead.

The spiritual outpouring at the Kirtland Temple was a sacred time in Church history. It reminded the Saints that God was with them and that the Restoration was real.
