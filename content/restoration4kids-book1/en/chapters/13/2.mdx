### Lesson 13.2 -- What He Restored

When you look at everything Joseph Smith brought back to the earth, the list is staggering. Through him, the Lord didn't just restore one or two things. He restored everything needed for the salvation and exaltation of God's children. Joseph was the **instrument**. The Lord was always the **source**.

First, the **priesthood** was restored. John the Baptist brought back the **Aaronic Priesthood**, and Peter, James, and John restored the **Melchizedek Priesthood**. Without priesthood authority, no ordinance could be performed with God's approval. This was the foundation everything else was built on.

Second, the **Book of Mormon** came through Joseph's translation. This sacred record is another testament of Jesus Christ, written by ancient prophets and preserved for our day. It answers questions that had confused Christians for centuries and testifies powerfully that Jesus is the Christ.

Third, Joseph received the doctrines of **eternal families** and the **sealing power**. Through temple ordinances, husbands and wives and children could be bound together not just until death, but for **all eternity**. This was one of the most glorious truths ever revealed.

Fourth, **temple ordinances** were restored: the endowment, baptism for the dead, and sealings. These ordinances made it possible for every person, living or dead, to receive the blessings of the gospel. No one would be left out.

Fifth, the full **organization of the Church** was established, with apostles, prophets, seventies, bishops, and other callings, just as Christ had organized His Church anciently. This wasn't a church designed by men. It was structured the way the Lord directed.

Beyond these, Joseph restored the **plan of salvation** in its fullness, the **Word of Wisdom**, the law of **tithing**, and organized **missionary work** to take the gospel to the world. He produced more **published revelation** than any other prophet in recorded history. The **Doctrine and Covenants**, the **Pearl of Great Price**, and the **Book of Mormon** all came through him.

Think about that for a moment. One man, one young, uneducated farm boy, was the means by which all of this came to earth. Not because Joseph was the source of any of it, but because the Lord chose him and he was willing to serve. Every time you take the sacrament, attend the temple, read the Book of Mormon, or feel the power of the priesthood, you are benefiting from what the Lord restored through Joseph Smith.
