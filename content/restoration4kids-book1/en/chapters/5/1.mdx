### Lesson 5.1 -- The Angel Moroni Appears

Three years passed after the First Vision. Joseph Smith was now **seventeen years old**. He had been persecuted for telling people what he had seen, and he felt concerned about his standing before God. Had he made mistakes? Was the Lord still mindful of him? On the night of **September 21, 1823**, Joseph knelt beside his bed and prayed to find out.

What happened next was extraordinary. A **light** appeared in his room, growing brighter and brighter until the room was lighter than noon. Then a **personage** appeared, standing in the air above the floor. He was wearing a brilliant white robe, and his whole being seemed to glow.

The visitor said his name was **Moroni**. He was an **angel** -- a resurrected being who had once lived on the earth. He told Joseph that **God had a work** for him to do. Joseph's name would be known for good and evil throughout the world.

Moroni explained that there was a **book written on gold plates** buried in a hill not far from the Smith family farm. The book contained an account of the **former inhabitants of the American continent** and the **fulness of the everlasting gospel** as the Savior had delivered it to those people. Buried with the plates were two stones set in silver bows, fastened to a breastplate, called the **Urim and Thummim**. God had prepared these stones for the purpose of **translating the book**.

Moroni then quoted many scriptures from the Bible, including prophecies from **Isaiah, Joel, and Malachi**, explaining how they were about to be fulfilled. He warned Joseph to keep his mind focused on bringing forth God's work and not to use the plates for personal gain.

Here is one of the most remarkable parts of the story. Moroni did not come just once that night. He came **three separate times**, repeating the same message each time with small additions. Joseph was awake the entire night. By morning he was exhausted, but he understood clearly what God wanted him to do.

Three visits in one night. The message was that important. God wanted to make absolutely sure Joseph understood every detail.
