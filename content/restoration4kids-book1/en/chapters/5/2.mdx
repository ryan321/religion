### Lesson 5.2 -- The Hill Cumorah

The day after Moroni's three nighttime visits, Joseph Smith was working in the fields with his father. He was so exhausted from being awake all night that his father noticed and told him to go home. On the way, Joseph collapsed near a fence. While lying there, Moroni appeared to him **again** and repeated everything from the night before. This time, Moroni told Joseph to go tell his father about the visions and then go to the hill.

Joseph obeyed. He told his father everything, and his father believed him completely. He told Joseph this was from God and to do exactly what the angel had said.

Joseph went to the **Hill Cumorah**, located about **three miles south** of the Smith family farm. He recognized it immediately from the visions Moroni had shown him. Joseph climbed the hill and found the spot. On the west side, near the top, he found a **large stone** covering a box made of stones cemented together.

Joseph pried the stone up with a lever and looked inside. There they were -- the **gold plates**, the **Urim and Thummim** (the two special stones for translating), and a **breastplate**. They were real. Everything Moroni had described was right there in front of him.

Joseph reached for the plates, but **Moroni appeared again** and stopped him. Joseph was not ready yet. He needed more **preparation**. Moroni told Joseph to come back to this exact spot **one year from that day** and that he should return every year until the time was right.

This must have been hard for Joseph. He could see the plates. He knew the work God wanted him to do. But he had to **wait**. He was only seventeen, and God knew he needed more time to grow, to learn, and to become the kind of person who could handle such a sacred responsibility.

The Hill Cumorah would become one of the most important places in the history of the Restoration. But for now, it was a place of patience and preparation. Joseph would have to come back year after year, trusting God's timing instead of his own.
